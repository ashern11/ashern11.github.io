FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Vendor

Mover provides an excellent delivery solution from you placing an order to the final delivery to your customer at the best and lowest shipping rates from our top-notch courier partners. With just few clicks on your electronic device, your selected courier partner will pick up your parcel from your home or workplace and deliver it for you. All orders are processed automatically with the Carrier through Mover website.
Mover courier partner include Nationwide Express and will have more joining in soon.

Just make your delivery order online at www.mover.my and your selected courier will pick up from your place. These are the ordering steps:

  • Step 1: Log in to your account
  • Step 2: Check rates by keying in the delivery postcode and item weight
  • Step 3: Choose your PickUp Date and your preferred Courier service.
  • Step 4: Key in your delivery details and click “Next”.
  • Step 5: Confirm your booking and click “Check Out”.
  • Step 6: Once order is confirmed, click on the “Pay with Credit Now” button. For new account, you’ll have SGD25 credit upon sign up to try delivering for free.
  • Step 7: Print out the air waybill, attach to your parcel and wait for parcel collection.

Customer

  • Step 1 – At the Quote bar, key in delivery postcode and item weight
  • Step 2 – Click on “Search” button

If you are happy with the Quote, click on the “Go Deal” button to continue your transaction.

The rates are inclusive of Fuel & Handling surcharge and Government Tax. Additional 6% GST charges will be imposed on your top up amount.

There are three payment methods:

  • Bank Payment
  • Crypto Currency
  • Senang Pay
